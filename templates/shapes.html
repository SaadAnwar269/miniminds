Learn Shapes!

Circle
Circles are round and have no corners. Think of the sun or a clock!
Square
Squares have 4 equal sides and 4 right angles — like a checkerboard tile!
Triangle
Triangles have 3 sides — they can look like a mountain or a slice of pizza!
Rectangle
Rectangles have 2 long sides and 2 short sides — like a book cover.
Star
Stars shine bright in the sky and have points that sparkle!
Oval
Ovals are like stretched circles — like an egg or a balloon.
Heart
Hearts are a symbol of love. You'll see them on Valentine's Day cards!
Hexagon
Hexagons have 6 sides. Bees use them to build strong honeycombs!
